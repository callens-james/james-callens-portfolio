# Safe AI Manifesto

## Principle
Use AI as a **force multiplier**, not a sole decision-maker of record.

## Commitments
1. Human-in-the-loop for risky actions
2. Local-first defaults where practical
3. Explicit gates for paid APIs and high-risk operations
4. Full decision traceability and audit logs
5. Fail-safe behavior under uncertainty/timeouts
6. Rollback readiness before automation rollout

## Why this matters
AI improves speed and reach, but unchecked automation can magnify errors. Safe AI practices keep reliability, accountability, and operational trust intact.
