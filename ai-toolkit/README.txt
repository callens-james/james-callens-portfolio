────────────
Day 1–2: Open the Prompt Library. Read all prompts so you know what tools you have. Use the 30/60/90 Day Plan Builder prompt right away. Day 3–14: Begin stakeholder interviews. Open the Process Audit Template for every manual workflow you observe. Fill one out per process. Week 3–4: Transfer your findings into the Prioritization Matrix. Score each process. The dashboard will auto-rank them. Week 5–6: Present your ranked list to your manager as your first 30-day deliverable. Month 2: Set up the Data Pipeline tracker with real data from the sustainability team. This becomes your recurring monthly input tool. TIPS FOR YOUR FIRST CORPORATE ROLE
────────────────────────────────────
1. Listen more than you talk in the first 30 days. Your role is to understand before you change anything. 2. Document everything. Use the Process Audit Template even for processes that seem simple — patterns emerge over time. 3. Never send AI output directly. Always review, verify numbers, and personalize. You are accountable for what you send. 4. Build relationships before building tools. The best automation fails if people don't trust or adopt it. 5. Connect your work to 's published goals: carbon neutrality, responsible AI, UN SDGs. Use their language, not generic consulting language. 6. Save copies of your outputs.
